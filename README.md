# Monthly SKU Profit & Pricing Analysis

## Objective
Analyze SKU-level pricing and profit data to optimize pricing strategy and improve overall profitability.

## Dataset
Columns include: Index, SKU, Style ID, Catalog, Category, Weight, TP, MRP Old, Final MRP, Amazon MRP, Flipkart MRP, Myntra MRP, Paytm MRP, Snapdeal MRP, Profit MRP, Profit Percentage, Price Consistency, Price Band.

## Tools Used
- Excel (Data cleaning, preprocessing, analysis)
- Python (Visualization, insights, calculated metrics)
- Power BI (Interactive dashboard)

## Process
1. Cleaned and processed raw SKU data in Excel.  
2. Added calculated columns and conducted visual analysis in Python with chart-wise insights and recommendations.  
3. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with KPIs, slicers, and charts for decision-making.  

## Power BI Dashboard

**Heading:** SKU Profit & Pricing Dashboard  

**KPIs:**  
- Total Profit: 2M  
- Average Profit %: 74%

**Slicers:**  
- Price Band (High, Medium, Low)  
- Price Consistency (Consistent, Inconsistent)

**Charts:**  
1. Profit by Category – Column chart  
2. Profit vs Weight by Category – Scatter chart  
3. SKU Count by Price Consistency – Pie chart (46% consistent, 53.64% inconsistent)  
4. Profit Trend by Weight – Line chart  
5. Profit Share by Price Band – Donut chart (High: 19.3%, Medium: 80.59%, Low: 0.04%)  
6. Profit Contribution by SKU – Column chart  

## Key Insights & Recommendations
- Focus pricing strategies on medium and high profit SKUs to maximize returns.  
- Improve price consistency across platforms to maintain competitiveness.  
- Optimize SKU weight and pricing relationship to enhance profitability.  
- Monitor low-performing SKUs for potential pricing or product adjustments.
